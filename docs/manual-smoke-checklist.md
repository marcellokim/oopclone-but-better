# Manual Smoke Checklist

1. Configure and build the project with CMake.
2. Launch `territory_war`.
3. Verify nation select works by click and by keys 1-4.
4. Hover multiple tile types and confirm owner / troops / terrain / capital info appears.
5. Issue 25%, 50%, and 100% orders with keys 1/2/3 + mouse clicks.
6. Confirm friendly moves merge troops and enemy moves trigger combat / tile capture.
7. Watch at least one AI nation defend, expand, and counterattack during a live match.
8. Confirm capital troops regenerate faster after gaining more territory.
9. Confirm the game ends when the player loses their capital or when only one nation remains.
10. From the result screen, press Enter or left click to return to nation selection.
