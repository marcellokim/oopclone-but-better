# Manual Smoke Checklist

1. Configure and build the project with CMake.
2. Launch `territory_war`.
3. Verify nation select works by click and by keys 1-4.
4. Confirm the four doctrine cards render the English display names `Sogang Univ.`, `Hanyang Univ.`, `Sungkyunkwan Univ.`, and `Chung-Ang Univ.`.
5. Hover plains / road / highland / mountain / sea / capital tiles and confirm owner / troops / terrain / capital info appears.
6. Issue 25%, 50%, and 100% orders with keys 1/2/3 + mouse clicks.
7. Build Command Power, press Space, and confirm the ability panel shows active / cooldown / notice states.
8. Click the ability panel and confirm it activates the same ability path without selecting map tiles.
9. Confirm friendly moves merge troops and enemy moves trigger combat / tile capture.
10. Confirm sea tiles reject normal land routes and mountain-heavy routes launch fewer troops than road routes.
11. Watch at least one AI nation defend, expand, counterattack, and spend a faction ability during a live match.
12. Confirm capital troops regenerate faster after gaining more territory, and SKKU ability visibly boosts regen.
13. Confirm the game ends when the player loses their capital or when only one nation remains.
14. Confirm the debrief screen sorts standings by score and shows score, grade, troops, tiles, and ability usage.
15. From the result screen, press Enter or left click to return to nation selection.
